RECAPPING & MAINTENANCE
NEXUS:1/hardware/maint> ALERT: CAPACITOR LEAKAGE
— Most A500/600/1200 machines are now 30-40 years old.
— Electrolytic capacitors fail over time, leaking corrosive fluid.
— Symptoms: distorted audio, screen flicker, random crashes, failure to boot.

NEXUS:1/hardware/maint> RECOMMENDED ACTION
— Recap ALL boards before sustained use. Non-negotiable on A600/A1200.
— Use SMD tantalum or Panasonic FR-series electrolytics.
— Resources: AmigaKit sells recap kits. EAB has model-specific cap lists.
